Butter Chicken

A rich and creamy tomato-based curry with tender pieces of marinated chicken.

Ingredients

  1. 500g boneless chicken
  2. 200ml tomato purée
  3. 100ml cream
  4. Spice mix (garam masala, turmeric, chili)
  5. Butter and oil

Masala Dosa

Crispy fermented rice pancake filled with spiced potato masala — perfect for breakfast or dinner.

Ingredients

  1. Dosa batter (rice & urad dal)
  2. Potatoes
  3. Mustard seeds & curry leaves
  4. Onion and turmeric
  5. Ghee or oil for frying

Palak Paneer

A comforting spinach curry with cubes of soft paneer — hearty and nutritious.

Ingredients

  1. 300g spinach
  2. 200g paneer
  3. Garlic, ginger, and green chili
  4. Ground spices (cumin, coriander)
  5. Butter or oil
